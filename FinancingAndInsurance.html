Auto Loan FAQ

If I have bad credit, can I still finance my purchase through Holiday Automotive?

Holiday Automotive works with all types of credit! After filling out a financing application, which can be done here, our finance team will to set up a time to go over your options. If Holiday Automotive financing isn’t the way you want to go, we do work with most third party lenders as well.

How does Holiday determine financing terms and rates?

Your Holiday Automotive Financing terms are based on your credit history, yearly income, and the price of the vehicle you’re looking at. Since vehicle price is a factor, you will notice that your terms will vary depending on the price of the vehicle.

Am I able to pay more than the required amount for my monthly payment without incurring a penalty?

You are absolutely able to pay more than your required monthly payment. There is no penalty for paying off the loan early, and you will save on interest charges by doing so.

What are my options if I can’t afford the down payment?

Our primary goal at Holiday Automotive is to set you up for success in your loan. Your lowest possible down payment will vary depending on the vehicle you choose. You can also trade-in your current vehicle to help lower your down payment.

Can I add a co-signer to my vehicle?

Absolutely, this is a great option if your credit is less than perfect. If you have a cosigner with really good credit, then you may be able to circumvent higher interest rates.

I want to finance with my bank/credit union. How does that work?

During the purchase process, you will be asked to provide the name of your bank or credit union, the amount of your loan, and your loan officer’s name and contact information, if applicable.

Once you complete the purchase process and schedule for delivery, our underwriting team will provide you the necessary documents to forward to your bank or loan officer to finalize your loan. Please note that your loan must be funded and we will need to verify the method of payment with your financial institution before we can confirm your delivery or pickup.
