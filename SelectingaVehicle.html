Selecting a Vehicle

Driving a new car is fun, why should buying it be any different?

how to develop your own website
  1. NEEDS ANALYSIS - your product specialist will get to know you to figure out which vehicles best meet your list of wants and "must haves," and of course, your budget.
  2. WALKAROUND - with the options narrowed it's time to get up close and personal with the vehicle. Your product specialist will demonstrate all of the vehicle features and answer any questions you have.
  3. TEST DRIVE - the first half of the test-drive your product specialist will drive allowing you to experience the ride without the distraction of traffic. Then halfway, she will swap places with you and it's your turn to drive.
  4. TRADE EVALUATION - if you are looking to trade-in your current vehicle we will offer free appraisals whether you end up selling to us or not. Our offer is based on today's market value of the vehicle and you can apply the value towards your desired vehicle.
  5. SIMPLIFIED BUYING: Our customers informed us that they didn't want to go through the hassles and traps that they normally associated with car buying. So we listened and developed our Simplified Buying approach. Every single vehicle at Holiday Automotive is discounted up-front to give you our best, lowest price right away, without having to haggle or negotiate to get our best offer. It's that simple!
  6. FINALIZING YOUR PURCHASE: Once you are satisfied with the terms, your product specialist will introduce you to one of our knowledgeable finance managers who will walk you through all of the paperwork. They will also explain your vehicle's warranty and will complete any necessary paperwork should you choose to take advantage of any of our extended service plans.
