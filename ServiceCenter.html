SERVICE CODE OF ETHICS

Holiday Automotive operates under a strict code of ethics. All employees are taught to follow this strict code of ethics

  1. RECOMMEND corrective and maintenance services and explain to the customer which of these are required now to correct existing problems and which are for preventive maintenance.
  2. OFFER customer a price estimate for work to be performed.
  3. OBTAIN prior authorization for all work done, in writing or by other means satisfactory to the customer.
  4. MAKE every effort to keep customer informed about appointments and completion deadlines.
  5. PROVIDE and clearly explain itemized invoice for parts and services.
  6. TREAT our customer’s property with the utmost care while it is being serviced.
  7. FIX IT RIGHT THE FIRST TIME This means listening closely to guests to properly understand  understan the best to fix the customer’s vehicle right the “first time.”
  8. UPHOLD the high standards of our profession and seek to correct any or all abuses within the automotive industry.
